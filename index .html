Coping Toolkit
Evidence-based. Any one of these works. Use it.
🔍
HALT Check-In
›
When a craving hits — which of these is actually driving it?
H
HUNGRY
Low blood sugar amplifies every urge. Eat something now.
A
ANGRY
Name it. Text someone. Don't sit with it alone.
L
LONELY
Connection is the antidote. One text. Do it.
T
TIRED
Sleep deprivation spikes relapse risk. Rest IS recovery.
🌊
Urge Surfing
›
The craving is a wave. It will peak — then fall. Ride it.
1
Sit with it. Don't run. Don't fight. Just observe.
2
Find it in your body. Chest? Gut? Name the sensation.
3
Rate it 1–10. Most urges peak at 15–20 min then fade.
4
Breathe through it. Inhale 4 · Hold 4 · Exhale 6.
🌱
5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
›
Anchors you in the present. Interrupts the spiral. Do it now.
5
5 things you SEE. Name them out loud.
4
4 things you TOUCH. Feel the texture.
3
3 things you HEAR. Background sounds.
2
2 things you SMELL. Even faint smells.
1
1 thing you TASTE. Your mouth right now.
🧠
SOBER Space
›
5-step pause. 3 minutes. Clinically proven for high-risk moments.
S
Stop. Whatever you're doing. Right now.
O
Observe. What am I feeling? No judgment.
B
Breathe. One deep breath in, slow out.
E
Expand. You are more than this moment.
R
Respond. Choose one recovery action.
🔄
If You've Already Used
›
You're not starting over. You're continuing.
1
Get somewhere safe. Don't be alone. Call someone now.
2
Don't stack shame. One slip doesn't erase your progress. It's data, not identity.
3
Call 988 or your sponsor. Right now. This is what they're there for.
